Context  permalink

Feb 2021: I was working within a medium sized interdisciplinary team. My involvement as the sole UX designer in the team consisted of conducting user research, the design and creation of the program application form, and utilising Zapier to create key automations. Although my work in terms of UX/UI within this stage of the project was minimal, it was crucial for my understanding of the recruitment / hiring process and for what was to come.
